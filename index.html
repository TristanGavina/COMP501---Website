Use of Generative AI assistance for higher education

Defining the topic

Artificial Intelligence (AI) has evolved rapidly in the past 5 years, advancing in neural networks, speech recognition, natural language processing and more (Littman et al., 2021), which led to the creation of Generative AI as we know it today. Since the development of Generative AI models using GPT, students can now receive immediate feedback on questions relating to course concepts and ideas, which when used as a tool, can solidify students understanding and knowledge. Mao (2023) defines Generative AI as a system “which utilizes machine learning algorithms designed to respond to prompts and generate new context across various formats, including text, audio, images, code, simulations, and videos” (p. 58). Currently, researchers in academia and education are assessing the educational and ethical implications that Generative AI has in assisting student learning and assessment work.
